Baking Tips

The first baking tip is if you are just starting baking ease into it. You want to start by doing simple things like cake or cookies, and you also want to follow the recipe as precisley as you can so you have a better chance at succeding. One more tip that I have is to try and use wheat flour instead of all purpose flour as all purpose flour is horrible for you and wheat flour is better for you, this will help you stay healthy even if you bake alot.
